Who We Are

We are Max Veyder and Oscar de Pins, two students passionate about finance, investing, and entrepreneurship.

Why We Started

We founded our Finance Club because we believe financial literacy is one of the most important life skills a person can develop, yet it is rarely taught in schools. From budgeting and saving to understanding taxes, investing, and financial markets, these are skills that can have a lasting impact on future success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to teach financial literacy to younger students in an engaging, accessible, and practical way. We want to help students build confidence in managing money, understand how the financial world works, and develop habits that will benefit them throughout their lives.

How We Teach

Through interactive lessons, discussions, quizzes, and real-world examples, we make complex financial concepts easy to understand. Whether students are learning about personal finance for the first time or are already interested in economics and investing, our goal is to create an environment where everyone can learn, ask questions, and grow.

Our Vision

As founders, Max and Oscar share a strong interest in finance and business. We created this club to bridge the gap between what students learn in the classroom and the financial knowledge they will need in the real world.

We believe that financial education should start early, and we are committed to helping the next generation make smarter financial decisions and build a stronger future.
